  1. Deal: Everyone gets 7 to 10 cards (depending on how many players). Put the rest face down (the Draw Pile). Flip one card face up (the Discard Pile).
  2. Start your turn: You MUST take a card. You can take the top one from the hidden Draw Pile OR the top one from the face-up Discard Pile.
  3. Meld (Optional): If you have a group (Set or Run), you can place it face up on the table. You can also add cards to groups other players played!
  4. End your turn: You MUST throw away one card onto the Discard Pile.

Brief History

Rummy is a family of card games that started in the early 1900s. It is played all over the world and has many versions. Rummy is famous for its easy rules and the fun of making sets and runs!
